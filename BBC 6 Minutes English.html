Do you like the crunch of biting into a fresh apple? What about the crunch of biting into a roasted insect? Georgie and Beth discuss this and teach you some new vocabulary.

This week's question

According to a 2024 YouGov study, which is the most popular type of apple in the UK?

  • a) Pink Lady
  • b) Gala
  • c) Red Delicious

Listen to the programme to hear the answer.

Vocabulary

  • nothing better in the world: used to emphasise how much someone loves or enjoys something
  • one of life's true pleasures: a particular experience, activity, or feeling that someone finds very enjoyable and satisfying
  • a ton of (something): a lot of (something)
  • mushy (food): soft (food)
  • convert: person who has changed their beliefs or way of living
  • snack: small bit of food you eat between meals, like crisps or a chocolate bar
